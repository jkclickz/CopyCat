ABOUT US

Copycat is a cutting-edge creative and tech-driven studio specializing in 2D & 3D animation, game development, web development, VFX, motion graphics, hand-drawn art and video editing. We bring ideas to life with precision, innovation and automation, ensuring high-quality results that meet industry standards.

Our mission is to blend creativity with technology, delivering solutions that are not only visually stunning but also highly functional. Whether it’s crafting immersive animations, developing engaging games, designing seamless web experiences, or producing cinematic VFX—we turn imagination into reality.
